Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 29, 2026
Math Kids - Brainstorming Math Skill ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information.
1. Children’s Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K)
This App is designed for children. We strictly comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
•
We do not knowingly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) from children.
•
We do not require users to enter any personal information to use the math quiz features.
2. Information Collection and Use
•
Offline Play: The core math quizzes are designed to be played offline. We do not track your location or access your contacts.
•
Local Data: High scores and progress (stars/badges) are stored locally on your device using an internal database. This data stays on your device unless you clear the App's cache.
•
AI Features: The App uses Google Gemini AI to enhance learning prompts. This process is designed to be anonymous and does not link your identity to the AI queries.
•
Firebase: We use Firebase for basic app stability and performance monitoring. This does not involve collecting personal user data.
3. No Advertising
Math Kids - Brainstorming Math Skill is a Paid App. We do not include any third-party advertising, trackers, or commercial "in-app purchases" that could distract or target children.
4. Third-Party Services
While we do not collect data directly, the App uses the following Google services which have their own privacy policies:
•
Google Play Services
•
Google AI / Gemini
•
Firebase Crashlytics
5. Security
The security of your information is important to us. Because we do not collect personal data, there is no risk of your personal identity being stolen through this App.
6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
7. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
